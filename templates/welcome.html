Open Speech Recording

This is a fork of the original audio crowdsourcing tool made by Pete Warden, which was used to put together Speech Commands, an open source keyword data set. I just changed it and simplified it to save the audios locally, with the intent to use them for keyword spotting tinyml applications.
Any information or audio recorded here is sent to anywhere but the same computer where it is running.
I strongly suggest you to take a look at the awesome work made by Pete and very well described in the Speech Commands paper.
